Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 10, 2025

T.A. Engineering ("us", "we", or "our") operates the https://www.taengineering.com/ website (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from our visitors.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Welcome to the TA Engineering website. We are a leading provider of defence engineering and manufacturing services, and we are committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning information on our website.

Our website serves as an informational platform to showcase our services, projects, and company profile. We do not require visitors to provide personal information to access our site.

1. What Information We Do Not Collect

TA Engineering does not actively collect any personally identifiable information from its visitors. This means for our informational website:

  • We do not have contact forms that ask for your name, email address, or phone number.
  • We do not require user registration or accounts.
  • We do not use tracking cookies for marketing, advertising, or personalized content.
  • We do not collect financial or transactional data.

2. What Information May Be Passively Collected

Like most modern websites, our web server may automatically log standard, non-personally identifiable information for technical security and analytical purposes. This information is not used to identify individual visitors.

  • Log Data: Your Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, and pages visited on our site. This data is used for website traffic analysis and to maintain the security of our server.
  • Essential Cookies: Our website may use essential cookies provided by our web host for basic site functionality. These cookies do not track your activity across other websites and do not store any personal information.

3. How We Use This Information

Any passively collected, non-personal information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To maintain and improve the performance and security of our engineering services website.
  • To analyze website traffic and understand how visitors interact with our content, which helps us improve the user experience.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties.

4. Links to Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, such as our valued clients and partners. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of these other sites. We encourage our users to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personally identifiable information.

Information Collection and Use

Our website is for informational purposes only. We do not use cookies for tracking, and we do not have any forms or other methods for you to provide us with personal data directly on this website.

Log Data

Like many site operators, we may collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our Service ("Log Data"). This Log Data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, browser version, the pages of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other statistics. However, this data is collected in an aggregated and anonymous form and is not used to identify you personally.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to help us operate our website. These services may have their own privacy policies. The third-party services we use are:
