About Our Firm

At the heart of India's 'Make in India' and 'Aatma-Nirbhar' initiatives, TA Engineering provides the critical engineering backbone for the nation's most vital defense and naval projects. Our mission is to deliver mission-critical systems with unwavering precision and reliability, transforming complex designs into operational reality. We are not just service providers; we are strategic partners in building a self-reliant India.

With a legacy of over 17 years of proven experience, TA Engineering has grown from a focused vision into a national asset. Headquartered in Thane, Maharashtra, our primary activity is providing high-stakes engineering services, specializing in the technical testing, analysis, and integration of complex systems where performance and reliability are non-negotiable.

Our work is a testament to our deep expertise in architecture and engineering. We function as a crucial link in the defense supply chain, ensuring that every component we assemble and every system we integrate meets the most stringent standards of quality and performance, solidifying our role as a trusted name in India's engineering landscape.
